	CPU - Central Processing Unit

		The CPU is the brain of the PC (where the actual computing is done).The type
		and family of the CPU have a major info on our OS as programs are desinged to 
		work on specific types of processors.

		in the desktop computer world there are 2 main types of CPU x86 and x86-64

		the CPU is inserted in the motherboard (with the pins downwards)


		32-bit and 64-bit processors

		64-bit processor was released by intel for the first time (was initially designed
		for servers)

		64-bit is usually better/faster

		32-bit is usually limited to 4gb rams of internal memory, 64 bit can support more
